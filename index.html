Have you been told that “us-maersk” is shipping a pet to you?
Do you have a receipt from “us-maersk” covering payment for a puppy or for shipping? If so, this is a SCAM!
us-maersk is an association with professional pet shippers. us-maersk does NOT work with other associations; our members ship pets using just our company name.
There is no legitimate pet shipping company working with “us-maersk” or “us-maersk” in their name.
If you see a company using the us-maersk name, please report it to petscams@us-maersk.org
Scammers victimize American consumers at an alarming rate, read the recommendations by the Better Business Bureau (BBB).
